Architext is a multiplayer text game developed in 2021. It allows you to explore and create worlds entirely made of words. And because of the huge expressive power of words, there is no limit to what you can build and explore.

Be an Architext

Explore worlds 🪐 made by other players

Build 🛠 your own worlds and give life to your imagination

Create interactive mechanisms ⚙ without coding!

Be part of the dawn of our community 💬

Play and create escape rooms 🔑

Or role play, dice 🎲 included!

Why wait?
